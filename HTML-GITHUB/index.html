FORZA HORIZON 5
Forza is a racing video game series forXbox consoles and Microsoft Windows published byXbox Game Studios. The franchise has sold 16 million copies as of December 2016 and has garnered critical acclaim. The franchise is primarily divided into two ongoing titles.

Team up and enter the Horizon Arcade to enjoy a series of fun, over-the-top challenges. Meet new friends in Horizon Tour or compete and take on the world in Horizon Open across Races, Drift, Games and The Eliminator – Forza Horizon’s unique take on battle royale. Blend in with traffic and sneak to your destination in Hide & Seek and explore a hand-picked collection of some of the community’s favorite Evolving World updates in Horizon Realms, each with their own Skill Score leaderboard.
